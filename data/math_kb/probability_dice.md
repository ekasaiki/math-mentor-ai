A fair dice has 6 equally likely outcomes:
{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}

Total outcomes = 6

Prime numbers on a dice:
{2, 3, 5}

Even numbers:
{2, 4, 6}

Odd numbers:
{1, 3, 5}

Example:
Probability of getting a prime number =
Number of favorable outcomes / Total outcomes
= 3 / 6 = 1/2
