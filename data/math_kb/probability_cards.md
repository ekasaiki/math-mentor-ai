A standard deck has 52 cards.

Breakdown:
- 4 suits: Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, Spades
- 13 cards per suit
- 12 face cards (King, Queen, Jack)
- 4 Kings
- 26 red cards

Probability formula:
Probability = favorable outcomes / total outcomes

Example:
Probability of drawing a King = 4 / 52 = 1 / 13
